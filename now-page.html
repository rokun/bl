now

Inspired by Derek silvers, here is a list of what I'm currently working on. Of course, I'm always open to interesting collaborations, so if you've got an idea than please feel free to reach out!

01

Creating meaninful Inclusion with Belong

We live in a world that is more connected than ever, yet more divided than ever. Belong is my attempt to change that. We're a team of creatives who use design-thinking to try and bring about more diversity within the creative community

02

User Experience / User Interface Freelancing

I'm working with a number of clients to help bring their ideas to life. My past clients include Nike, Nickelodeon and a whole host of startups. If you're interested in working with me, please get in touch and let's make your idea happen!

03

Building a Diversity-Focused Sketch Plugin

As designers, we have the ability to design the change we want to see in the world. I believe we need to be more representative in our work, so I'm working on an open-source plugin that makes it easy for designers to use more representative assets in their mockups.

04

Writing for TheNextWeb, CreativeBloq & more

I was fortunate enough to be given the opportunity to share my thoughts on how we can use what I call 'diversity design' to create a more inclusive community. You can see my articles coming out shortly!

05

Filming Different By Design

Different by Design is my designs how that teaches creatives how to 'level up' their creative work through the use of reserach-backed insights. It's filmed on location, and is for the designer and creative-thinker alike. Subscribe over at Youtube for the next episode

06

Building an Inclusion Framework

Over the years I've been fortunate enough to travel to over 60 countries, and now am bringing these cross-cultural insights into a guide designed to help designers create more inclusive products and services.

07

Writing a book on Pitching

As a former actor turned successful startup founder, I'm distilling the insights gleaned raising 3 rounds into an action-focused book aimed at helping entrepreneurs raise pitch their ideas. It launches in January 2017.

08

100 day fitness challenge

A few years ago I lost 100lbs though clean eating and training. Then I got complacent. Although I havent put the weight back on, I feel like this journey is unfinished and so on my 33rd birthday I set myself a new challenge. You can read about it here.
